What are the different types of hypnosis?


Hypnosis is a technique that has been used for centuries to help people overcome various issues and improve their well-being.   • Clinical Hypnosis
    • Clinical hypnosis is a type of hypnosis used to treat various medical and psychological conditions.     • Ericksonian hypnosis is a type of hypnosis that uses indirect suggestion and storytelling to access the subconscious mind. It is named after Milton H.   • Stage Hypnosis
    • Stage hypnosis is a type of hypnosis that is performed for entertainment purposes. It involves a hypnotist inducing a trance in a group of people and then performing various suggestions and commands. Stage hypnosis is not recommended for therapeutic purposes and should only be performed by trained professionals.

    • Self-hypnosis is a type of hypnosis that can be practiced by individuals on their own.
